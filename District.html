Kerala, a state located in the southern part of India, is known for its natural beauty and rich culture. Each district has it's own beauty and style.
Here are the districts that is must to visit.

Allapuzha

One of the most beautiful and popular districts in Kerala is the district of Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha. This district is located on the southwestern coast of Kerala and is known for its picturesque backwaters, beaches, and network of canals.

Pathanamthitta

It is known for its religious significance, natural beauty, and cultural heritage. The district is home to a number of temples, churches, and mosques, making it a popular pilgrimage destination for people of different faiths.


More info
Wayanad

The district is known for its natural beauty, wildlife sanctuaries, and cultural heritage. Wayanad is located in the Western Ghats, a mountain range that runs along the western coast of India, and is home to several forests, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries.

More info

Idukki

The district is known for its picturesque beauty, diverse flora and fauna, and rich cultural heritage. It is situated in the Western Ghats, a mountain range that runs parallel to the western coast of India.The district is famous for its natural attractions like hills and mountains.

More info
Kollam

The district is known for its natural beauty,historical landmarks,and cultural heritage.It is situated on the banks of the Ashtamudi Lake, which is the second-largest lake in Kerala and is home to several backwater channels.

More info
Thrissur

It is a district located in the central part of the state.The district is known for its rich cultural heritage,vibrant festivals and historical monuments.Thrissur is the fifth largest city in Kerala and is home to several iconic landmarks and attractions.
